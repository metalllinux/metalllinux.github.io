---
title: "How Completely Remove Microk8S"
category: "kubernetes-and-containers"
tags: ["kubernetes-and-containers", "completely", "remove", "microk8s"]
---

microk8s stop
microk8s disable
sudo snap remove microk8s
sudo rm -rf /var/snap/microk8s/
sudo rm -rf /var/snap/microk8s-common/
sudo rm -rf /etc/kubernetes/
