* To find any command, do `Meta + x`
* To find files, that is `Space + .`
* `Ctrl + w v` for vertical split.
* `Ctrl + w c` to close a split.
* `Space + b k` to kill the current buffer.
* `.doom.d/init.el` has all of the modules you can enable.
