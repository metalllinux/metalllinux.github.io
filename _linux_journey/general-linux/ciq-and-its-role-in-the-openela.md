There are two teams we are working with in OpenELA, the Technical Steering Committee and the Communications team (Marketing). The Comms team is made up of Cathey.co, CIQ Marketing, OSPO, Marketers from SUSE and Oracle. Comms is currently working on a website redesign. I'm on tap for building the website once the structure is sorted out. The TSC is working on a handful of projects with technical staff from SUSE, Oracle, and CIQ OSPO. The main project being worked on is ELvalidated (a compatibility tool for RPM packages across Enterprise Linux distros). OSPO currently has no formal role or authority in the TSC, but we are collaborating to build compatibility XMLs and running the TSC meetings (which are not always regularly attended). Art is the key player from CIQ's side but Greg is also involved.
