**Event:** Tokyo Linux Users Group (TLUG) monthly meetup
**Date:** 9 May 2026
**Audience:** Japanese + English — the deck is bilingual (EN / JA)

A short 10-minute talk on **Project TV — Rocky Linux Edition**, a Rocky-Linux-on-Kubernetes home media stack with Mirakurun, EPGStation, Jellyfin, Tube Archivist, Navidrome, Grafana and ZFS + sanoid. Covers the one-command installer, the DKMS-packaged `px4_drv` TV-tuner driver, recommended hardware (PLEX PX-W3PE5 + SCM SCR3310 B-CAS reader), and why Kubernetes was the right fit for this home lab.

**Repository:** [metalllinux/project-tv-rocky-linux-edition](https://github.com/metalllinux/project-tv-rocky-linux-edition)
